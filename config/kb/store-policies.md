# Order Tracking

You can track your order status by logging into your account and visiting the
"My Orders" page. Each order shows its current status: Processing, Shipped, or
Delivered. Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of shipment.

# Shipping Policy

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 5-7
business days. Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99.
Next-day shipping is available for $19.99 on orders placed before 2 PM EST.

# Return Policy

Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must
be unused and in original packaging. To initiate a return, visit "My Orders" and
select "Return Item." Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we
receive the item.

# Exchanges

Exchanges are available for items of equal or lesser value. To request an
exchange, initiate a return and place a new order for the desired item. If the
exchange item costs less, the difference is refunded to your original payment
method.

# Product Warranty

All electronics carry a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Warranty claims can be
filed through our support team. Proof of purchase is required. The warranty
covers manufacturing defects but does not cover physical damage or water damage.

# Contact Support

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.
You can reach us via live chat on our website, email at support@store.example.com,
or phone at 1-800-555-0199. Average response time for email inquiries is 4 hours.
